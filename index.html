Biography

I am a PhD student at the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Fudan University, Shanghai, China, supervised by Prof. Cuiwei Yang. I received my M.Eng. from Fudan University and my B.Eng. from the University of Shanghai for Science and Technology.

My research interests lie in the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare, with an emphasis on Medical AI, Multi-Agent Systems, Multimodal Foundation Models, and Cardiovascular Research.

I am actively looking for research collaborations. Please feel free to reach out via email if you are interested.
